Meet Your Groomer

Hi, I'm Tatyana — and I love what I do.

I'm a pet groomer with 5+ years of professional experience, and I grew up working with rescues and fostering pets in need of homes. My special interest is in skin and coat health — because your pet deserves to not only look their best, but to feel their best, too.

Every pet that visits me gets the full treatment — gentle handling, premium products, and the patience each one deserves. I use industry-backed techniques to keep even the most anxious pups calm and comfortable from the first brushstroke to the final blow-dry.

Rain or Shine Pet Grooming was built on a simple belief: your pet's happiness and well-being put a smile on my face. Your furry friend is in good hands — and will leave looking and feeling their absolute best.

— Tatyana Strahan LaBlue
